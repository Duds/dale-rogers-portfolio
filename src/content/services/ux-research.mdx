## Our Research Approach

We believe that effective UX research balances:

- **Rigor**: Using proven research methodologies and analysis techniques
- **Relevance**: Focusing on questions that directly impact business outcomes
- **Practicality**: Delivering actionable insights that can be implemented within real-world constraints

### Tailored Research Methods

Depending on your specific questions and constraints, we employ a variety of research methods:

#### Qualitative Methods

- In-depth user interviews
- Contextual inquiry (observing users in their environment)
- Usability testing (moderated and unmoderated)
- Diary studies
- Focus groups
- Card sorting and tree testing for information architecture

#### Quantitative Methods

- Surveys and questionnaires
- Analytics analysis
- A/B testing
- Benchmark studies
- Heatmap and clickstream analysis
