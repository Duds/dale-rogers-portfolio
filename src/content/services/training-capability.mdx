# Training & Capability Building

<UserCallout type="info">

Empowering your teams with the right skills and mindset is crucial for driving successful digital transformation and delivering excellent services. We offer tailored training programs, workshops, and coaching to build internal capability in design thinking, user research, service design, and digital ways of working.

## Building Sustainable Internal Skills

True digital maturity comes from within. We focus on building your team's internal capability, moving beyond reliance on external consultants. Our training covers essential skills in design thinking, user research methodologies (from interviewing to usability testing), service design principles, and integrating these practices within agile and government contexts. We equip your teams not just with knowledge, but with the confidence to apply it.

## Flexible Learning Formats

We understand that different teams have different needs. We offer a range of learning formats, including immersive multi-day bootcamps, targeted skills workshops, ongoing team coaching, and executive briefings. All programs are customized based on initial needs assessments and emphasize hands-on activities, real-world case studies, and practical application to ensure learning sticks.
